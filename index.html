school achievements

INTERNATIONAL KARATE CHAMPIONSHIP

Five students from school bagged total 9 (4 gold, 2 silver & 3 bronze) medals at the 5th International Karate Championship 2019 organised by All India Seishinkai Shitoryu Karate-Do Federation.

ATL MARATHON WINNERS

Two science projects by young innovators of the school made it to top 100 all over India at ATL Marathon - 2019 organised by Niti Aayog and Atal Innovation Mission. At 3rdrank was ADBHUT- The smart packaging system crteated by Himanshu Sharma, Ashwin Barnwal and Ayush Bansal of Class X while project Smart Bin by Arnav Gupta (XI) and Ananya Gupta (IX) ranked 87th .

F1 IN SCHOOLS WORLD FINALS

‘Team Matadors’ stood second runners up at the world finals of 15th ‘F1 in Schools’ competition held at Abu Dhabi. Amitians represented India for the second consecutive year in the prestigious international STEM competition for school children. The team comprised team leader Sukriti Gupta (XI) and team members Jaisal Kothari (IX), Vivaan Wadhwa & Aaryan Goyal (XI) and Swetabh Changkakoti & Noor Sharma (XII), who competed against 60 teams from over 25 countries.
